The Concept

What's a misogi?

Misogi (禊) is a Japanese Shinto practice of purification — originally, standing beneath a freezing waterfall to wash away the old and start clean. Modern adventure culture borrowed the idea: pick one challenge a year that's genuinely hard, hard enough that finishing isn't guaranteed, and take it on anyway. We think most people go soft without something like it — a mission that demands everything and hands you back the feeling that you're actually alive. That's the spirit behind every Charlie Mike objective. Got one in mind? Pitch it to the crew — it might just become the next mission.

Some people need a challenge to feel alive. Are you one of them?
